# 💧 HydroHero

HydroHero is a **gamified water conservation and hygiene platform** that encourages people to adopt sustainable water habits through challenges, rewards, and community participation.

Users complete daily water-saving tasks, earn points, unlock achievements, and contribute to their community’s total water savings. The platform also estimates the **real-world water impact** of user actions.

HydroHero supports **Sustainable Development Goal 6 – Clean Water and Sanitation.**

---

## 🚀 Features

### Water-Saving Challenges
Daily tasks designed to promote responsible water usage.

Examples:
- Turning off tap while brushing
- Taking shorter showers
- Reusing water for plants
- Using buckets instead of hoses

### Gamification System
Users earn:
- Points
- Badges
- Achievement levels

Leaderboards encourage friendly competition between communities.

### Water Impact Calculator
The system estimates water saved based on completed tasks.

Example:
- 5-minute shorter shower → ~50 liters saved
- Using bucket instead of hose → ~70 liters saved

### Trust Score System
To reduce cheating, verification methods such as the following can be used:
- Photo upload
- Quick quiz verification
- Task completion tracking

### Community Leaderboards
Communities compete based on total water saved, encouraging large-scale participation.
